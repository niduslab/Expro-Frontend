# Pension Role Application - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Quick Start

### For Members (Users)

#### Step 1: Navigate to Role Application
1. Login to your dashboard
2. Click **"Apply for Role"** in the sidebar (Award icon)

#### Step 2: View Available Roles
You'll see:
- Your current role (e.g., General Member)
- Available roles you can apply for:
  - **Executive Member** - 60,000 TK (requires payment)
  - **Project Presenter** - Free
  - **Assistant Project Presenter** - Free

#### Step 3: Apply for a Role
1. Click on the role card you want to apply for
2. Fill in the application form:
   - **Application Reason** (required) - Explain why you want this role
   - **Supporting Documents** (optional) - Upload PDF, JPG, JPEG, or PNG files
3. Click **"Submit Application"**

#### Step 4: Payment (Executive Member Only)
If applying for Executive Member:
1. After submission, status will be "Payment Pending"
2. Click **"Pay Now"** button
3. Complete payment via bKash (60,000 TK)
4. Status automatically changes to "Under Review"

#### Step 5: Wait for Approval
- Admin will review your application
- You'll receive a notification when approved/rejected
- If approved, role is automatically assigned to you!

---

### For Admins

#### Step 1: Navigate to Applications
1. Login to admin panel
2. Click **"Role Applications"** in the sidebar (Award icon)

#### Step 2: View Dashboard
You'll see:
- Total applications
- Payment pending count
- Under review count
- Approved/Rejected counts

#### Step 3: Filter Applications
Use the filter bar to:
- Search by application number or member name
- Filter by status (Payment Pending, Under Review, etc.)
- Filter by role type (Executive Member, PP, APP)

#### Step 4: Review Application
1. Click the **eye icon** to view details
2. Review:
   - Member information
   - Application reason
   - Payment status (if applicable)
   - Supporting documents

#### Step 5: Approve or Reject

**To Approve:**
1. Click the **green checkmark** icon
2. Add review notes (optional)
3. Click **"Approve"**
4. Role is automatically assigned to member!

**To Reject:**
1. Click the **red X** icon
2. Enter rejection reason (required)
3. Add additional notes (optional)
4. Click **"Reject"**
5. Member is notified with reason

## 📋 Role Requirements

### Executive Member
- **Fee:** 60,000 TK
- **Payment Method:** bKash
- **Requirements:** 
  - Must be General Member
  - Payment must be completed before approval
  - Application reason required

### Project Presenter
- **Fee:** Free
- **Requirements:**
  - Must be General Member or Executive Member
  - Application reason required
  - No payment needed

### Assistant Project Presenter
- **Fee:** Free
- **Requirements:**
  - Must be General Member or Executive Member
  - Application reason required
  - No payment needed

## 🔄 Application Status Guide

| Status | What it Means | What to Do |
|--------|---------------|------------|
| **Payment Pending** | Waiting for payment | Complete payment via bKash |
| **Under Review** | Admin is reviewing | Wait for admin decision |
| **Approved** | Application accepted | Role assigned! Check your profile |
| **Rejected** | Application declined | View rejection reason, can reapply |
| **Cancelled** | You cancelled it | Can submit new application |

## 💰 Payment Process (Executive Member)

### Step-by-Step Payment

1. **Submit Application**
   - Fill form and submit
   - Status: "Payment Pending"

2. **Click "Pay Now"**
   - Button appears in your application card
   - Initiates payment process

3. **bKash Payment**
   - Redirected to bKash payment page
   - Enter mobile number
   - Enter PIN
   - Confirm payment of 60,000 TK

4. **Automatic Processing**
   - Payment confirmed
   - Status changes to "Under Review"
   - Wallet transaction recorded
   - Admin can now review

5. **Admin Approval**
   - Admin verifies payment
   - Approves application
   - Role assigned to you!

## ❓ FAQ

### For Members

**Q: Can I apply for multiple roles at once?**
A: No, you can only have one pending application at a time.

**Q: Can I cancel my application?**
A: Yes, you can cancel applications with status "Payment Pending" or "Under Review".

**Q: What if my payment fails?**
A: Contact support. You can retry payment from your application.

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 1-3 business days after payment (for Executive Member) or submission (for other roles).

**Q: Can I reapply if rejected?**
A: Yes, you can submit a new application after addressing the rejection reason.

### For Admins

**Q: Can I approve without payment?**
A: No, Executive Member applications require completed payment before approval.

**Q: What happens when I approve?**
A: The role is automatically assigned to the member in the system.

**Q: Can I bulk approve applications?**
A: Yes, use the bulk approve feature for multiple applications.

**Q: How do I verify payment?**
A: Payment status is shown in the application details. Look for "Paid" badge.

**Q: Can I edit an approved application?**
A: No, approved applications cannot be modified. Contact support if needed.

## 🆘 Support

### Common Issues

**Issue: Can't see "Apply for Role" in sidebar**
- Solution: Make sure you're logged in as a member (not admin)
- Check if you have an active pension enrollment

**Issue: "Pay Now" button not working**
- Solution: Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact support if issue persists

**Issue: Application not submitting**
- Solution: Check all required fields are filled
- Ensure file size is under 5MB
- Try removing and re-adding documents

**Issue: Can't approve application (Admin)**
- Solution: Verify payment is completed (Executive Member)
- Check application status is "Under Review"
- Refresh the page and try again
